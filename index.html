ABOUT THE CAFE

The Cafe was purchased by Jim and Della Yates in June of 1989. Jim a certified executive chef had worked in the valley at restaurants such as The Pointe Resort, Tony Romas and later co-owned Michaels Fountain View.

We serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff.

"Nothing like Homemade bisquits and gravy."

Chef and Owner: Jim Yates

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 1pm.

Address: 36457 N Cave Creek RD, Cave Creek AZ 85331
