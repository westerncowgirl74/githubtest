ABOUT THE CAFE

Chef and Owner: Jim and Susan Yates

The Cafe was purchased by Jim and Della Yates in June of 1989. Jim a certified executive chef has worked in the valley at restaurants such as The Pointe Resort, Tony Romas and later co-owned Michaels Fountain View. Jim and his Daughter Susan currently run the cafe together and can both be seen cooking in the kitchen Jim on weekends and Susan during the week.

We serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch.

Our Sausage gravy is made from scratch fresh everyday!!

Our homefries are from freshly cooked and cut potatoes NO FROZEN POTATOES HERE!!

"Nothing like Homemade bisquits and gravy."

Opening hours: Monday Thru Friday 6am to 12:45pm Saturday and Sunday 7am to 12:45pm
